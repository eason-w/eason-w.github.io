About Me!

15y/o Innovator, Coder, and Writer

Hello! I'm a 15 year old student in Ottawa, Canada who is super interested in exponential technologies and how I can use them to make a positive impact in a world full of problems. In particular, I am interested in the various fields of AI and also sustainability tech like Cellular Agriculture.
